Get the widget !

Why for ?

The widget allows you to display in your website the rank of a conference or a journal.

You can also use it to display other information, such as the h-index or the number of published articles, when available.

How can I use it ?

You just have to include one line of javascript in your page.

<script src="http://cwid/ID"><script>

and replace "NAME" with the name of the conference or journal.

Examples

Simple usage

If you just want to add the rank badge, the following line is all you need

<script src="http://confer.org/widget/45"><script>
